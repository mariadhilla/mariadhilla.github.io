Amazon

Redesigning a global transportation system

Background

Over the summer in 2022, I interned at Amazon in Seattle, WA. There, I led the redesign effort for TORCH, an internal change management system in Global Transportation Technology Services (GTTS).

The main components of my internship were as follows:

  • Interviewing 15+ users and developers to create user personas
  • Co-designing and leading a 4-day workshop in Nashville to extract a north star for the redesign as well as insights about the different ways employees used TORCH internationally
  • Analyzing our findings to develop information architecture diagrams and user flows
  • Delivering a full scale Figma redesign of TORCH using an internal style system

If you'd like to know more, let me know; I'd be happy to elaborate on my project!
